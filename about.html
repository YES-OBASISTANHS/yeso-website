About YES-O

Empowering youth to become responsible environmental stewards through leadership, service, and sustainable action.

🌱 History of YES-O

The Youth for Environment in Schools Organization (YES-O) is a student organization that promotes environmental awareness, conservation, and sustainable practices among learners. Through environmental campaigns, volunteer activities, and leadership opportunities, members become active partners in protecting nature within the school and the community.

🌎 Vision

A community of environmentally responsible youth committed to building a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.

🎯 Mission

To educate, inspire, and empower students through environmental education, leadership, volunteerism, and meaningful environmental action.

Core Values

🌱 Environmental Stewardship
🤝 Leadership
💚 Service
♻ Sustainability
🌍 Teamwork
⭐ Integrity

Objectives

Environmental Awareness

Promote awareness through seminars, campaigns, and school activities.

Community Participation

Encourage volunteerism through clean-up drives and tree planting.

Youth Leadership

Develop student leaders who advocate for environmental protection.

📜 Legal Basis

YES-O supports environmental education initiatives in schools and serves as a student organization that promotes environmental awareness, conservation, and participation in school and community environmental programs.
